Whether you are an educator or a corporation - International Storytelling Ltd can help bring a richness and diversity to your organisation.  With over 25 years experience helping international schools, companies and governments.

As a modern company, you will recognise the need to manage communication and knowledge. International Storytelling Ltd offers expertise to help you achieve this. Including -  professional development, narrative management, corporate storytelling, presentation coaching and speech writing.

Good educators have long recognised the importance of storytelling in building key literacies, both oral and written. As a teacher, you will find International Storytelling Ltd will exceed your expectations in delivering high-quality learning opportunities. These programmes include traditional folk and fairy tales, medieval re-tellings, plus biographical and historical material. We offer storytelling performances for all levels from kindergarten to tertiary. Workshops in writing and storytelling for year 4 to tertiary levels. Workshops for staff on using storytelling in the curriculum.

Andy Wright is the company's principal storyteller a world-class performer having spent over 25 years telling stories to audiences in schools, theatres, and festivals throughout Europe, South America, Asia, Australia and New Zealand.

Andy is committed to helping children, adults, and organisations communicate better, discover their own stories, celebrate their own culture and the culture of others.
