About

International Storytelling Ltd is a company dedicated to communication and cultural understanding – utilising the latest advances in digital storytelling and information technology to make this happen. We have worked in 50 different countries over the last 30 years helping companies, governments, educators, and individuals to extend their communication skills and to build technology infrastructure to support better communication. We have helped to link cultures and communities around the globe using the latest technologies. Helping teams and individuals to understand that narratives, knowledge and communication are more than just written words. That the narratives we tell are a manifestation of a living symbiotic relationship between an audience and a teller, changing and growing as we grow and develop.


International Storytelling Ltd provides a wide range of services in the communication and information technology sphere - using an associate model, bringing in specialised practitioners to offer a wide range of services. Including: Information Technology training and design, Narrative and Knowledge Mangement, Writing Services, Professional Development, Storytelling and Speech Making.

Narrative Management

International Storytelling Ltd has helped many companies, organisations and individuals to succed through the judicial application or narrative management tools and skills. We can help you with the crafting and disseminating of compelling stories that shape organizational culture, drive change, and influence stakeholder perceptions. Helping you in understanding the narratives that already exist within an organisation and intentionally creating new ones to achieve strategic goals.

Infrastructure & Technology

International Storytelling Ltd works with a wide range of information technology specialists to provide implementation and training in all aspects of information management and infrastructure.

We have a special interest in opensource solutions to complex problems and can help to streamline and improve all aspects of your information systems. We regularly work with technology trusts, training providers and educational organisations to provide skills in all aspects of technology use and advanced coding.

Speech Writing & Coaching

International Storytelling Ltd can help you develop speeches and presentations for any situation or occasion. Our storytellers are all experts in the art of oratory and can provide speeches that are written to the highest standard, with careful consideration of the difference between written language and oral language. We can support individuals and groups in learning the skills of oratory and how to present their speeches or presentations in an optimum format.

Museum Interpretation Services

International Storytelling Ltd can help with the development of modern, interactive interpretive services sharing the stories of Aotearoa New Zealand. Incorporating research, writing, storytelling and encouraging others to tell their stories, we can work with museums and heritage organisations to design interpretive panels, AV and interactive digital exhibits.

Storytelling

International Storytelling Ltd has more than 30 years of storytelling experience across 50 countries. Our storytelling performances are interactive, sensory, and captivating, using sound, action, and spoken word to enchant audiences

Our storytelling offerings extend beyond performances; storytelling is the beginning of literacy. Before we can read and write our stories, we need to know and tell them.

International Storytelling Ltd conducts workshops for adults, students and managers, focusing on discovering and narrating personal and business stories. These workshops enhance storytelling skills, foster self-expression and grow creativity.

Professional Development

International Storytelling Ltd offers a wide range of professional development opportunities. We can help to place quality communication at the centre of your organisation. Providing professional development in all aspects of communication both digital and analogue.

We are experts in digital storytelling and all the associated technologies. Storytelling is a tried-and-tested method in education, be it by writing a book, oral storytelling, or through presentations. We know that societies have used storytelling to teach key principles throughout millennia. It is no surprise that storytelling has been used as an information medium in education of all types, including in dentistry, general medicine, law, and business.

Thanks to the technologies of the 21st century, storytelling has taken on a new form. It has become richer with the incorporation of digital media like videos, audio files, as well as images — a method called digital storytelling. International Storytelling Ltd can help you to make the most out of your digital storytelling resources and align it with seamlessly with your narrative.
