Welcome to a world where spoken words inspire us to be the best that we can be.

World renowned orator, storyteller and spoken-word artist Andy Wright invites you to explore the power of the spoken word as you learn and experience the power of oratory and storytelling.













Storytelling Performances and Programmes

Andy Wright is a world-class performer having spent over 30 years telling stories to audiences in schools, theatres, and festivals throughout Europe, South America, Asia, Australia and New Zealand.


"Andy was absolutely fabulous. The students and teachers thoroughly enjoyed his humour and the exquisite deliveries of the three contrasting stories. Andy is a true storyteller who enters into the heart of his stories and has his audience in the palm of his hand" (St Clement of Rome School)

Learning Centre - International Storytelling school of oratory.

Discover the power of your own unique voice.  Learn how to be a great orator. Reveal the powerful and important story that you have to tell.

International Storytelling Ltd offers a series of educational programmes for all ages from school-children to business CEO's helping us all to unleash the power of oratory. Choose from 10 week advanced courses or 1 week intensive courses. Leveraging the power of modern technology we offer courses around the globe with video-conferencing and Learning Management systems fully integrated into our courses
